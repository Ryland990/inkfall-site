Inkfall

A survival roguelite where every spell is a brush stroke you draw yourself — rendered in a living sumi-e ink-wash style.

iOS 17+ iPhone No ads No energy timers

About

You play a calligraphy-brush spirit, fighting off swarms of ink blots across escalating waves. There are no buttons for spells — every attack is a real gesture traced on-screen: a slash, a spiral, a figure-eight. Land a big enough stroke and an ink dragon rides the exact path you drew, tail dissolving as it fades — a payoff shaped by your own hand movement, not a canned animation.

Survive to the boss fight, then keep going in Endless mode. A Brush Codex lets you unlock new gesture forms between runs, and Game Center leaderboards (including a daily seeded challenge) track your best.

Features

  • Gesture spellcasting — draw the spell, don't tap a button for it
  • Ink-wash art direction — the whole battlefield is painted, not textured
  • Dragon's Tail — a signature move literally shaped by your stroke
  • Brush Codex — meta-progression that unlocks new forms between runs
  • Game Center — leaderboards plus a daily seeded challenge run
  • No ads, no energy timers — built by one person, not a live-ops team
