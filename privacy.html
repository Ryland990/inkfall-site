Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Inkfall ("the app") is developed and published by Razvan Popa ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data we collect

None. Inkfall does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. We have no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, and no account system.

Data stored on your device

Your gameplay progress, unlocked forms, settings, and local run history are stored only on your device (via Apple's standard on-device storage) and are never sent to us. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center for leaderboards, that sign-in and the scores you submit are handled entirely by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy. We never see your Game Center data directly — Apple's Game Center service processes and displays it.

In-app purchases

If the app offers an in-app purchase, it is processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information — Apple handles all billing under its own privacy and payment terms.

Children's privacy

Inkfall does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal data at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes (for example, if a future update adds an optional analytics or account feature), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
